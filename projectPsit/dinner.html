Is it good to eat dinner?
It is untrue that late night snacks or eating in the evening will cause significant weight gains. This is because evening calories are the same as daytime calories. You only gain weight if your caloric intake is more than your caloric expenditure during the day.

When it comes to dinner, overeating or eating too much of the wrong kinds of food can lead to trouble sleeping. On the flip side, a meal that is less than satiating can leave you wanting more and result in reaching for an unhealthy late-night snack even closer to bedtime.
